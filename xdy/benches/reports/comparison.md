## Regressions (3 cases)

All regressions involve deeply right-nested expressions with repeated
subexpressions. The likely cause is the common subexpression elimination (CSE)
optimizer pass encountering a different AST structure from the nom parser,
leading to more work. Flat equivalents of these expressions show the typical
50%+ improvement.

| Expression | tree-sitter | nom | Change |
|------------|-------------|-----|--------|
| `x: ({x} * ({x} * ({x} * (1 * (1 * 1)))))` | 19.52 µs | 61.99 µs | +217.6% |
| `x: ({x} + ({x} + ({x} + (1 + (1 + 1)))))` | 25.11 µs | 74.64 µs | +197.3% |
| `x, y: (({x} + {y}) + ({x} + {y})) + (({x} + {y}) + ({x} + {y}))` | 26.29 µs | 32.08 µs | +22.0% |
